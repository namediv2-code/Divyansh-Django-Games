Cricket rules

First you choose either to bowl or
bat first.If you are batting first,
then enter a number from 1 to 6.
Then the computer chooses a number from 1 to 6 aswell.


If both the numbers are equivalent, you are OUT.
If NOT, your input will be added to your runs.


If you are bowling first, it is the same thing but with
roles reversed. There are 2 innings which means if you
bowl or bat first, you have to bat and bowl
second(respectively).


Whether you bat or bowl
first, your goal is to get more runs than
the computer in the end.
